About the Exam

What is the CSE-PPT?

The Philippine Civil Service Examination is the gateway to government employment eligibility. Hauers is built to help you pass it.

Two Levels, One Platform

Professional 170 items • 3h 10m • 2nd-level
Sub-Professional 165 items • 2h 40m • 1st-level

Both levels require an 80% passing score. Available as a Pen & Paper Test (PPT) or Computerized Examination (COMEX), administered nationwide by the Civil Service Commission (CSC).

2026 Schedule: March 8 and August 9, 2026.

About CSPC ACCESS

CSPC ACCESS stands for Academic Center for Continuing Enhancement Services for Students at Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges.

The center trains students and graduates for licensure examinations, eligibility exams, competency assessments, and TESDA certifications—including the CSE.

Hauers is built in collaboration with CSPC ACCESS, giving reviewees a structured, intelligent advantage.
